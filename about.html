About Us

Our comprehensive ratings system empowers consumers to know the impact of brands on the environment, labour and animals; and allow consumers to make informed decisions about where they shop and who they support. Our vision is to use the power of consumers’ choices to make fashion fair and sustainable.

What We Rate

People

We take into consideration a brands treatment of its workers across the supply chain. We do so by determining if policies are implemented to protect worker rights, which include a safe and healthy working environment, and freedom to join unions. We further consider whether a brand provides adequate wage, and promotes gender equality across the supply chain. We ensure to consider how well a brand implements these policies.

Animals

We consider how well a brand has generated its animal welfare policies. We identify the use and source of fur, leather, wool, shearling, and any other form of animal skin and hair. We further take into consideration whether a brand is committed to reduce or eliminate the use of animal products.

Planet

We take into consideration a brands ability to manage resources and its disposal, their policies set to reduce greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions and chemicals use. The types of materials (textile) used during production, and the packaging of these items are important to consider. Further the emissions generated from the supply chain are observed, and whether targets or initiatives are set to reduce these GHG emissions. Further a brands chemical use is observed to determine whether goals are set to reduce or eliminate the use of hazardous chemicals, and new measures are in place to adapt to plant based or water based dyes.
